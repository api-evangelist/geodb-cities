GeoDB now supports the concept of a place. A place is any populated area and can currently be one of the following types: An administrative level-2 district (ADM2) A city or town (CITY) An island (ISLAND) As part of this evolution, the following new endpoint operations are now available: Find Country Places Find Country Region Places Find Places Near City Find Places Near Division Find Places Near
