GeoDB now supports GraphQL queries. GraphQL is a relatively new technology, originally developed by Facebook as an efficient way of querying the complex relationships inherent in their social domain model. GraphQL's primary advantage over REST is the ability to get back exactly what you want - and only what you want - in a single request.
