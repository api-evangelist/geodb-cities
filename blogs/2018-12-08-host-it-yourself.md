Although GeoDB works pretty well as a hosted cloud service, you may require the kind of control and performance that can only come from managing your own dedicated in-house instance. We are proud to announce that this option is now available in the form of the new Self-Hosted tier. The Self-Hosted tier allows you to host your own dedicated GeoDB instance with no restrictions on number of requests.
